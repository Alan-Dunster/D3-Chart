Temperature and Air Quality in Cardiff

The data presented in this visualisation clearly shows pollution levels increase as temperatures fall, except for Ozone levels, which appear to do the opposite.

Chart 1 shows the variation of the daily measures from their annual averges (0% baseline). Comparing measures to a basline is useful when the scale range between some measures (i.e. Carbon Monoxide) is too small to compare easily with others (i.e. Nitric Oxide). Chart 2 represents the actual daily measures. Whilst the same trends can be observed by zoomimng in, this chart is made available so the viewer can see both perspectives.
